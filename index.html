Frequently Asked Questions

What is XRP? +
XRP is a cryptocurrency used for fast and cheap international transfers. It might play a bigger role in finance in the future.
How do I secure my crypto? +
Enable 2FA, use strong passwords, beware of phishing, and consider a self-custody or cold wallet for larger amounts.
What is diversification? +
Don't put all your eggs in one basket. Invest in different cryptocurrencies to spread risk.
